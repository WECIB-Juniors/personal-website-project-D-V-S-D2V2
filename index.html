About Me

My name is Darsh Pratap Singh, my email is dvps010@gmail.com, and my Instagram is king.d.v.s. I am 17 years old and currently attend Wake Early College of Information and Biotechnology. I was born on October 23, 2008, in India. I lived in Ohio for most of my life before moving to North Carolina in 8th grade.

After moving, I attended West Cary Middle School, where I applied to an early college program and was accepted. I chose to enroll at Wake Early College of Information and Biotechnology instead of my base school, Apex Friendship High School, to take advantage of the unique opportunities it offers in technology and biotechnology.
